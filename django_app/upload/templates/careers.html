Selection Criteria

What We Look For

We care far more about how people think and communicate than which technologies they've listed on a CV.

🧠

Deep Technical Thinking

You understand why things work, not just how to use them. You can reason about tradeoffs, failure modes, and scalability constraints.

📦

Production Experience

You've shipped systems that real users depend on. You know what it feels like when something breaks at 2am and what it takes to prevent that.

💬

Clear Communication

You can explain complex technical problems to non-technical stakeholders and write documentation that actually helps.

🔁

Continuous Learner

The landscape changes fast. You stay current, experiment with new tools deliberately, and know when not to use the shiny new thing.

🤖

AI-Fluent

You use AI tools as an accelerant for your own thinking — not as a replacement for it. You can evaluate AI-generated output critically.

🤝

Low-Ego Collaborator

You care more about shipping the right solution than being right. You give and receive direct feedback without taking it personally.
